## 👨‍💻 About me

I am an **AI, Big Data and Automation Specialist** with a solid foundation in IT Systems and Network Administration. I'm passionate about building, orchestrating, and automating complex processes and AI Agents, bridging the gap between advanced data architectures and efficient workflows.

I consider myself a versatile and proactive professional. My go-to language is **Python**, which I push to its limits for AI and data projects, while I also rely on **JavaScript and TypeScript** to program complex logic within automations. I have experience designing workflows that integrate various **relational and non-relational databases**, ensuring optimal memory management and context optimization for LLMs.

Beyond software, I am also a huge **hardware** enthusiast. I love building PCs from scratch and staying up to date with the latest industry trends, understanding the infrastructure powering the code.
